Key Features
  • Informative Product Pages: Clear descriptions of various compression stockings for conditions like venous leg ulcers, lymphoedema, and varicose veins.
  • Educational Content: Detailed guidance for specific conditions, including how the stockings improve circulation and reduce swelling.
  • Contact Portal: Direct contact details for inquiries and customer support
Development Process

The development process for ActiLEGS began with an in-depth understanding of the brand’s mission to provide high-quality therapeutic compression products supported by clear, reliable medical information. I started by designing a clean, clinical, and user-friendly interface that would enhance trust and ensure ease of navigation for customers. Using a responsive framework, I structured the site to feature detailed product descriptions, educational content for various medical conditions, and a compelling company profile that highlights ActiLEGS’s expertise and achievements. Content integration was handled with care—organizing condition-specific pages and visual elements to communicate both professionalism and compassion. The build phase was followed by rigorous testing for performance, accessibility, and responsiveness across devices. Finally, I implemented SEO best practices, deployed the site to the live server, and ensured ongoing support for content accuracy and technical stability.
