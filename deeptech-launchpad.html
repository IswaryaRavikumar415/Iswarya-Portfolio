Development Process

I began with requirement gathering by understanding that DeepTech Launchpad aims to bridge the industry-academia gap by training fresh graduates in deep tech domains. In the design phase, I crafted a layout that balanced professionalism and technical clarity, prioritizing user flow to encourage program discovery. The implementation phase involved building the platform using a responsive tech stack (front-end and backend), integrating a dynamic curriculum display, and providing secure career placement messaging. After that, I conducted thorough testing for UI/UX responsiveness and metadata correctness. Finally, I launched the platform, ensured fast load times, and streamlined the messaging of mission and vision for maximum brand clarity.
